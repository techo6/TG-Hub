THE TG STORY

FROM CONTENT TO CHAMPIONS

Total Gaming was born in 2018, built from the ground up by Ajju Bhai (Ajendra Variya) — one of India's most iconic gaming content creators. What started as a YouTube channel documenting Free Fire gameplay quickly evolved into a cultural movement across Indian gaming.

In August 2020, the esports division was officially launched by signing The Mafia's roster, marking TG's entry into competitive Free Fire. Since then, the squad has dominated India's biggest stages — winning the World Esports Cup 2021, the Esportz Invitational 2022, and sweeping both BR & CS titles at FFMIC 2025.

Revived stronger than ever in March 2025 after a brief hiatus, TG now fields one of the most feared rosters in South Asian esports. The mission remains the same: dominate every lobby, inspire every gamer.
